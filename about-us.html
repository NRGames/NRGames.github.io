NR Games is a game development company based in India since 2022, with offices in Assam, India and Vancouver, Canada.



What do we make?

Why do we make?

As gaming has become an important part of our life, we want to develop some games which will be played by millions of people and remembered forever.

What type of games do we make?

We make fun and enjoyable casual and hyper-casual games for mobile. But in future, we may create AAA quality PC games also.
